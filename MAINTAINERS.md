## Contact and Support

To maintain productivity and minimize unsolicited communications, we use the following channels for contact:

1. **Technical Support & Feature Requests**: Please use [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/tatsuki-washimi/gwexpy/issues) or [GitHub Discussions](https://github.com/tatsuki-washimi/gwexpy/discussions). This ensures that the community can benefit from the answers.
2. **Private or Sensitive Inquiries**: If you need to report a sensitive issue or have a request that cannot be discussed publicly, please use the following contact form:
